About

Beta Hypothesis is an independent research program by Alessandro Venca, reconstructing relativistic kinematics — and, in ongoing work, spin, mass, and electric charge — from the internal geometry of a fiber bundle.

Background

The program develops a sequence of results building on a single local geometric constraint, rather than on the standard postulates of special relativity. Every claim in the manuscripts is classified as Derived (proven from the postulates), Postulated (assumed as a starting axiom), or Open (an unresolved question) — see Open Questions for the current list.

Status

The first manuscript in the series is deposited on Zenodo (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.15706006) to establish public priority, and is currently under submission to a peer-reviewed journal. See Publications for the full record.

Approach

The work is independent — carried out without institutional affiliation — and is shared openly for scientific discussion, critical review, and comparison with related literature. Feedback and correspondence are welcome via the contact page.

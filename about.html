About

Beta Hypothesis is developed by Alessandro Venca. Independent research into geometic framing for relativistic dynamics and related topics.

Background

This project explores theoretical and computational approaches to relating local geometric constraints to emergent dynamical behavior. The site collects visualizations, open questions, and publications.

The dispersion cone and its mass-shell section

ω² = c²(kₓ² + k_θ²). A massive particle keeps k_θ fixed at 2π/λ₀ (the fundamental internal mode), so its state runs along the hyperbola where the plane k_θ = const cuts the cone.

At vₓ = 0 the point sits at the vertex of the hyperbola, on the ω axis at height ω₀ = m₀c² — pure rest energy. As vₓ increases it climbs the hyperbola (ω = γω₀, kₓ = βγ·k_θ), approaching the cone's edge — the photon line ω = c·kₓ — as vₓ → c.

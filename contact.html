About

The Beta Hypothesis is developed by Alessandro Venca, an independent researcher based in Switzerland.

The project investigates whether relativistic dynamics can be reformulated through a local geometric constraint involving an internal cyclic degree of freedom. The work is developed independently and is intended for critical scientific evaluation.

This website serves as a public reference point for the conceptual overview, mathematical structure, visualizations, manuscripts, and open questions of the framework.

Open Questions

The Beta Hypothesis is still under development. The following issues are intentionally stated openly.

Key unresolved points

  • Can all coordinate transformations be derived internally, without importing Lorentz transformations as an external postulate?
  • Can the framework reproduce every high-precision test of Special Relativity without hidden assumptions?
  • Can it generate distinct empirical predictions?
  • What is the precise physical status of the internal circular degree of freedom?
  • How should interactions and quantum-field-theoretic processes be formulated rigorously?
Principle: The goal is not rhetorical opposition to standard relativity, but a disciplined investigation of whether the same phenomenology can emerge from a different geometric foundation.
